## 🚀 How to Resubmit to AdSense

1. **Go to Google AdSense**: https://adsense.google.com
2. **Sign in** to your account
3. **Look for "Action needed" notification** → Click to review issues
4. **Read feedback** about the rejection
5. **Check all items in this checklist** are complete
6. **Click "Resubmit"** button
7. **Wait for review** (Usually 1-2 weeks)

### What Google Checks on Resubmission:
- Site has grown (More articles/content)
- Legal compliance (Privacy + Terms pages)
- Content quality improvement
- Proper monetization disclosure
- No policy violations
