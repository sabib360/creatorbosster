## Architecture Highlights

### Site Scale
- **8 Main Categories**
- **70+ Tools** (expandable to 100+)
- **30+ Core Pages** (expandable to 100+)
- **5 Content Clusters** with pillar content
- **4-Level URL Hierarchy** (clean, SEO-friendly)

### Navigation
- **Header:** Logo, Dashboard, Categories (dropdown), Blog, Guides, FAQ, About, Contact
- **Footer:** 4-column layout with Products, Resources, Company, Follow Us
- **Mobile:** Hamburger menu, sticky search, quick access to 5 top tools
- **Breadcrumbs:** On all pages for SEO and UX

### Internal Linking
- **Tool ↔ Blog Cross-linking:** Each tool links to 2-3 blog posts
- **Category Hubs:** Link to all tools in category
- **Related Items:** 3 items per page (tools, posts, categories)
- **Content Clusters:** 5 clusters with pillar content
- **Link Density:** 5-10% per page (healthy range)

### SEO Optimization
- **Keyword-Rich URLs:** /tools/[category]/[tool-name]/
- **Content Clustering:** 5 main clusters, each with pillar + 5 posts
- **Internal Linking:** Strategic cross-linking between tools and content
- **Breadcrumbs:** Navigation and schema markup
- **XML Sitemap:** Proper priorities and changefreq

### AdSense Strategy
- **8 Placements:** 4 on tools, 3 on blog, 1 footer
- **Daily Impressions:** ~4,700 (with 20-30k visitors)
- **Estimated Monthly Revenue:** $1,500-2,000 (scaling to $5k+ at 50k visitors)
- **Ad Types:** Mix of leaderboards, half pages, rectangles
- **Non-intrusive:** Strategic placement, good user experience
